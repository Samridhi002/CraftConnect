Frequently Asked Questions

When will CraftConnect launch?

We're working hard to bring CraftConnect to you soon. Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know about our official launch date!

How do I start selling on CraftConnect?

Once we launch, you'll be able to sign up for an account, verify your email, and start listing your handmade items. Our easy-to-use interface will guide you through the process.

What types of products can I sell?

CraftConnect will support a wide range of handmade and artisanal products, including but not limited to jewelry, pottery, textiles, woodworking, and digital designs.

How does the AI-powered matching work?

Our AI algorithm will analyze buyer preferences and shopping behavior to recommend relevant products and sellers, increasing the chances of successful sales and satisfied customers.

About CraftConnect

CraftConnect is your upcoming platform for connecting artisans and discovering unique handmade products. Our mission is to support local craftspeople and bring their products to a global audience.
