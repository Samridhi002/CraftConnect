Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start selling on CraftConnect?

Sign up for an account, verify your email, and start listing your handmade items. Our easy-to-use interface will guide you through the process.

What types of products can I sell?

CraftConnect supports a wide range of handmade and artisanal products, including but not limited to jewelry, pottery, textiles, woodworking, and digital designs.

How does the AI-powered matching work?

Our AI algorithm analyzes buyer preferences and shopping behavior to recommend relevant products and sellers, increasing the chances of successful sales and satisfied customers.

About CraftConnect

CraftConnect is your go-to platform for connecting artisans and discovering unique handmade products. Our mission is to support local craftspeople and bring their products to a global audience.
